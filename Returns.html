Our Simple Returns Policy

Your peace of mind is our priority. Since all our products are 100% authentic, returns are primarily for sizing issues or defects.

30-Day Return Window

You have 30 calendar days** from the date you received your item to request a return or exchange. Items sent back to us without first requesting a return will not be accepted.

Conditions for Return

  • Item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it.
  • Must be in the original packaging (including the shoe box, tags, and any included accessories).
  • The item must not show any signs of wear, scuff marks, or damage.

How to Initiate a Return

  1. Email us at returns@telebankicks.com with your Order Number and the reason for the return (e.g., "Size too small," "Defective stitching").
  2. We will review your request and, if approved, send you a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number and instructions on where to send the package.
  3. Upon receiving and inspecting the item, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund/exchange.

Refunds and Exchanges

Once approved, refunds will be processed to your original method of payment. Exchanges for a different size (if available) are processed free of charge. If the desired exchange item is unavailable, a full refund will be issued.

Items Not Eligible for Return

All "Limited Edition" or "Final Sale" items are not eligible for return, refund, or exchange, except in the case of a manufacturer defect.
