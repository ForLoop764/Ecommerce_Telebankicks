Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 7, 2023

TelebanKicks is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and disclose your personal information when you visit or make a purchase from our website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information you voluntarily provide to us when placing an order, registering for an account, or contacting us. This information may include:

  • Identifiers: Name, shipping address, billing address, email address, and phone number.
  • Payment Information: Details required to process transactions (we do not store full credit card numbers).
  • Browsing Data: IP address, browser type, device type, pages viewed, and referral source.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for various purposes, including:

  • Fulfilling Orders: To process your purchases, arrange for shipping, and provide you with invoices.
  • Communication: To send you updates regarding your order, or marketing emails if you opt-in.
  • Improving Our Site: To analyze how customers browse and interact with TelebanKicks to improve our service.

3. Sharing Your Personal Information

We share your personal information with third parties only to help us perform our services, such as:

  • Payment Processors: To securely handle payment transactions.
  • Shipping Carriers: To deliver your purchased items.
  • Analytics Providers: To understand website usage (e.g., Google Analytics).

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access the personal information we hold about you and to ask that your personal information be corrected, updated, or deleted. Please contact us using the details on our Contact page to exercise these rights.
