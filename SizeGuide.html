How to Measure Your Foot

  1. Place a sheet of paper on the floor against a wall.
  2. Step onto the paper with your heel lightly touching the wall.
  3. Mark the tip of your longest toe on the paper.
  4. Measure the distance from the edge of the paper (against the wall) to the mark in centimeters (CM).
  5. Compare your CM measurement to the chart above. Always round up if you are between sizes.

For specific fits (e.g., narrow or wide), please consult the product description or contact Customer Service.
