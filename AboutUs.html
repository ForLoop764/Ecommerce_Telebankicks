Our Story

It all started when our founder Samler Telebangco tried to buy limited edition sneakers online but ended up with fake "Nikay" shoes instead of Nike. Frustrated but inspired, he gathered his friends and said, "Why not create our own legit sneaker haven?" And thus, TelebanKicks was born where we do the sketchy online hunting so you don't have to! We partner with only 4 top brands, claim their products, and make sure you get the real deal without the "is this authentic or did I just get scammed" anxiety.
